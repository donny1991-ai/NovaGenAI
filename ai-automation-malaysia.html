What is AI automation for business?
AI automation uses artificial intelligence to handle business processes that currently require manual human work — processing documents, answering customer calls, qualifying leads, checking compliance, generating reports. Unlike traditional automation (which follows fixed rules), AI automation handles unstructured data, makes decisions, and adapts to new situations.
