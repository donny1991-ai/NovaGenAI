What's the difference between AI automation and RPA?
RPA (robotic process automation) follows fixed rules on structured data — it breaks when the input format changes, when a customer phrases something differently, or when an edge case appears. AI automation understands context, handles unstructured data, makes decisions, and adapts. RPA automates the predictable. AI automation handles the real world — which is rarely predictable.
