FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Does ChatGPT Enterprise keep our data private? What about Malaysian data residency requirements?

ChatGPT Enterprise provides tenant isolation — your data is not used to train OpenAI models. Enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit (AES-256, TLS 1.2+). Data residency options include regional data storage. We configure SAML/SSO so authentication stays within your identity provider. Audit logging captures every interaction. For organisations with stricter data sovereignty requirements, we layer ChatGPT Enterprise with on-premise AI infrastructure so sensitive data never leaves your building. We provide full security architecture documentation and can arrange a technical review with your compliance team before deployment.

What does ChatGPT Enterprise actually cost for a Malaysian organisation? Is the pricing transparent?

Two cost components: (1) OpenAI licensing — ChatGPT Enterprise seats are priced per user annually. We help you negotiate volume licensing directly with OpenAI. (2) NovaGenAI enablement — deployment and governance from RM 50,000, covering tenant provisioning, SSO integration, RBAC configuration, governance framework, and team training. Ongoing managed operations from RM 15,000/month. Every proposal includes a complete 3-year TCO breakdown — licensing, enablement, and operations. No hidden fees. No per-prompt charges. No surprise invoices.

We already use Microsoft 365. How does ChatGPT Enterprise work alongside Copilot?

They're complementary, not competing. Microsoft 365 Copilot excels inside the Microsoft ecosystem — summarising Teams meetings, drafting Word documents, analysing Excel data. ChatGPT Enterprise excels at broader reasoning, complex analysis, custom GPT creation, and cross-platform integration. We deploy both under a unified governance framework: same SSO, same RBAC, same audit logging, same data policies. Your employees use Copilot for Office-native tasks and ChatGPT Enterprise for everything else — custom workflows, strategic analysis, cross-system reasoning. One governance surface. Zero fragmentation.

How do you prevent employees from leaking sensitive data through ChatGPT prompts?

Three layers: (1) DLP guardrails — we configure prompt scanning and content filtering that detects and blocks PII, financial data, patient records, and classified information before it reaches the model. (2) RBAC and workspace segmentation — different departments see different GPTs and different data surfaces. Finance cannot access HR GPTs. Sales cannot access legal GPTs. (3) Full audit logging — every prompt, every response, every file upload is logged and searchable. If someone attempts to paste a customer database into a prompt, you know within minutes. Governance is not an afterthought — it's the architecture.

Your employees can log in and use ChatGPT Enterprise by day 5. Phase 2 — Governance & Custom GPTs (Weeks 2-3): DLP guardrails, audit logging, usage policies, and 2-3 department-specific custom GPTs with knowledge bases and API actions. Phase 3 — Training & Managed Handover (Week 4): Structured enablement across departments. Your teams are self-sufficient by day 30. Full production deployment in 14-30 days depending on complexity. We've done this across organisations of 50 to 500+ seats.
