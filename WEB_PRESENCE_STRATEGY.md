## NOTES & WARNINGS

### Press Release Writing Tips
- **Newsworthy:** Launch, funding, partnerships, major milestones
- **Format:** Headline, dateline, lead paragraph (who/what/when/where/why), body, boilerplate, contact
- **Length:** 400-600 words ideal
- **Links:** Include 2-3 contextual links to novagenai.com.my
- **SEO:** Include target keywords naturally (AI biotech, Malaysia AI, healthcare AI)

### Reddit Rules
- **NO direct self-promotion** — instant ban
- **Participate first, promote later** — 10:1 ratio (10 helpful comments for every 1 self-mention)
- **Read subreddit rules** before posting
- **Use "Founder" flair** for transparency
- **Provide value** — answer questions, share insights

### Stack Exchange Rules
- **High-quality answers only** — low-quality gets downvoted fast
- **No promotional content** — signature line only
- **Answer with expertise** — cite sources
- **Build reputation** before linking to own site

### Award Submission Strategy
- **Read criteria carefully** — tailor application to match
- **Provide evidence** — metrics, case studies, testimonials
- **Professional presentation** — polished deck, demo video
- **Follow up** — some awards allow revisions
