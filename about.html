// OUR STORY

Building the Future of Healthcare AI

NovaGenAI was founded with a singular vision: to bring enterprise-grade artificial intelligence to the healthcare and biotech industries in Southeast Asia.

Born from the intersection of deep AI expertise and real-world healthcare operations, we understand that this industry demands more than generic AI solutions. Patient data requires sovereignty. Clinical workflows demand precision. Regulatory compliance is non-negotiable.

That's why we build AI systems purpose-designed for regulated industries — deployed on-premise, multilingual by default, and compliant from the ground up.

Our Mission

To deploy intelligent AI systems that transform healthcare operations — making them faster, smarter, and more accessible — while maintaining the highest standards of data sovereignty and regulatory compliance.

Our Vision

A Southeast Asian healthcare ecosystem where every organisation — from stem cell banks to hospitals — has access to enterprise-grade AI that augments human capability and improves patient outcomes.
