Frequently Asked Questions

What is the AI Concierge Platform?

The AI Concierge Platform is NovaGenAI's flagship voice AI solution combining conversational AI with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). It handles customer enquiries, books appointments, qualifies leads, and provides 24/7 intelligent support — all with natural, human-like voice interactions.

What is Clarify?

Clarify is NovaGenAI's AI-native sales platform. It provides intelligent lead scoring, automated outreach sequencing, pipeline management, and real-time analytics — all powered by AI to help sales teams close more deals with less effort.

What is the Predictive Cell Model?

Our Predictive Cell Model uses computational biology and machine learning to predict cell viability and behaviour in biotech applications. It enables in-silico testing that reduces lab costs and accelerates research timelines.

Can NovaGenAI deploy AI on-premise?

Yes. We offer on-premise AI deployment using NVIDIA DGX Spark and custom infrastructure. This is ideal for organisations with strict data sovereignty requirements, especially in healthcare and financial services.

What is RAG Document Intelligence?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) Document Intelligence allows enterprises to query their own documents, policies, and knowledge bases using natural language. It combines large language models with your proprietary data for accurate, sourced answers.

How does NovaGenAI's Marketing OS work?

Our Marketing OS is an AI-powered marketing automation platform that handles content generation, campaign orchestration, audience segmentation, and performance analytics — enabling marketing teams to scale output while maintaining quality.
