FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What training formats do you offer? Can you train our team on-site?

Three formats: (1) On-site at your premises — our trainers come to you with all equipment, (2) Live virtual via secure video conference with hands-on lab environments, (3) At our Cyberjaya facility with access to production NVIDIA infrastructure. Every format is fully hands-on with real systems. We train teams from 5 to 200+. Multi-day programmes include pre-work, lab environments, and post-training assessments.

What are the prerequisites for your AI training programmes?

It depends on the programme. Executive Strategy Briefings require zero technical background — we teach AI strategy, not coding. ChatGPT Enterprise Training requires basic computer literacy. AI Engineering Bootcamps require intermediate Python and familiarity with command-line tools. Data Science Foundations requires basic numeracy. We conduct a pre-training skills audit for every cohort and assign participants to the right track. No one gets left behind.

Do you provide certification upon completion?

Yes. Every participant who completes a programme receives a NovaGenAI Certificate of Completion specifying the programme, duration, and competencies assessed. For AI Engineering Bootcamps, we issue a Capstone Certification upon successful deployment of a working AI system. These are practitioner certifications — they represent demonstrated capability, not attendance. Our enterprise clients value them as evidence of production-ready AI skills.

Can you customise the curriculum for our industry or specific use cases?

Absolutely. Our Custom Corporate AI Curriculum is built from scratch around your organisation's technology stack, data environment, regulatory context, and AI roadmap. We audit your team's current capabilities, interview stakeholders, and design modules that directly map to the AI systems you are building or planning. For healthcare: clinical AI, MOH compliance, patient data handling. For finance: BNM guidelines, fraud detection models, risk scoring. Training on your reality, not generic examples.

What post-training support do you provide?

Every programme includes 90 days of post-training support: (1) Dedicated Slack/Teams channel with your trainers for Q&A, (2) Monthly office hours — live sessions where participants bring real problems from their work, (3) Code and architecture review — submit your team's first AI projects and we review them against production standards, (4) Optional extended mentorship for teams building production systems. We measure success by what your team ships after training, not by what they heard during it.

How do we measure ROI on AI training investment?

We track four dimensions: (1) Pre- and post-training capability assessments — quantified skill improvement per participant, (2) 30/60/90-day application tracking — what AI projects have participants initiated or accelerated, (3) Time-to-productivity — how much faster does your team build AI after training vs. before, (4) Cost avoidance — what would it have cost to hire or outsource the capabilities your team now has in-house. We provide a structured ROI report at 90 days. Enterprise clients typically report 3-5x ROI within the first year through accelerated AI deployment and reduced external dependency.
