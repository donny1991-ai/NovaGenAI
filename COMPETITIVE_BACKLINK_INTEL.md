## Executive Summary

This report analyzes the backlink profiles and visibility strategies of Malaysian AI/tech companies to identify actionable link-building opportunities for NovaGenAI. Key findings:

1. **Directory Listings** are the foundation — Crunchbase, Tracxn, PitchBook, and regional tech directories provide high-authority backlinks
2. **Tech Media Coverage** via Tech in Asia, e27, Digital News Asia, and Malaysian tech outlets drives both backlinks and brand authority
3. **Government Grant Programs** (MDEC, Cradle) often publish recipient lists — free backlinks + credibility
4. **NVIDIA Inception** and similar tech accelerator programs provide directory listings and co-marketing opportunities
5. **Conference/Event Participation** creates backlinks from event organizer websites and media coverage
