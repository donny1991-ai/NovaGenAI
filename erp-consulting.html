Frequently Asked Questions

What ERP systems does NovaGenAI support?

NovaGenAI provides AI-powered consulting for all major ERP platforms including SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Cloud ERP, Microsoft Dynamics 365, and Odoo. We integrate AI agents and predictive analytics into your existing ERP environment — whether on-premise, cloud, or hybrid.

How do AI agents automate ERP workflows?

Our AI agents sit on top of your ERP and autonomously handle repetitive tasks — purchase order processing, invoice matching, inventory reorder triggers, financial reconciliation, and vendor management. They learn your business rules, execute within guardrails, and escalate exceptions to humans.

Can NovaGenAI deploy ERP AI on-premise?

Yes. Using NVIDIA DGX Spark infrastructure, we deploy AI models directly on your premises. Your ERP data never leaves your building — critical for organisations with strict data sovereignty, PDPA compliance, or air-gapped security requirements.

What is predictive analytics for ERP?

Predictive analytics uses machine learning models trained on your historical ERP data to forecast demand, optimise inventory levels, predict cash flow, identify supply chain risks, and flag financial anomalies before they become problems. NovaGenAI builds custom predictive models tailored to your specific business.

How long does an AI-powered ERP implementation take?

A typical AI-augmented ERP engagement starts with a 2-week discovery and assessment phase, followed by 4-8 weeks of phased implementation. AI agent deployment can begin delivering value within the first month, with predictive analytics models maturing over 8-12 weeks as they learn from your data.
