CryoCord Group

Malaysia's Largest Stem Cell Bank — Full AI Transformation

The Company

CryoCord Group is Malaysia's largest private cord blood and stem cell bank, commanding 65% market share. With over 160 employees and a multilingual customer base spanning English, Bahasa Malaysia, and Mandarin speakers, CryoCord needed AI solutions as diverse as their operations.

The Challenge

  • Manual, paper-heavy processes across departments
  • Multilingual customer base requiring support in 3 languages
  • Scaling operations without proportional headcount increase
  • Patient data sovereignty requirements under Malaysian PDPA
  • Sales pipeline visibility limited to spreadsheets

The Solution

NovaGenAI deployed a comprehensive AI transformation across CryoCord's entire operation — from customer-facing voice agents to back-office automation.

AI Voice Agents

Trilingual voice agents handling patient enquiries, appointment booking, and lead qualification — 24/7.

Clarify Sales Platform

Real-time sales intelligence dashboard with AI-powered lead scoring and pipeline automation.

HR Automation

Employee onboarding portal, policy Q&A agent, and document processing for 160+ employees.

Document Intelligence

RAG-powered knowledge base across SOPs, medical protocols, and compliance documentation.
