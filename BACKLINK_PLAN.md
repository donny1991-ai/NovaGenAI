## 🚨 Red Flags to Avoid

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Buy backlinks (Google penalty risk)
- ❌ Use link farms or PBNs
- ❌ Over-optimize anchor text (vary it naturally)
- ❌ Submit to low-quality directories (spam score risk)
- ❌ Ignore toxic backlinks (disavow if needed)
- ❌ Neglect social profiles after creation (post regularly)
- ❌ Duplicate content across guest posts
- ❌ Use exact-match anchor text excessively

**DO:**
- ✅ Focus on relevance over DA
- ✅ Build relationships, not just links
- ✅ Create genuinely valuable content
- ✅ Vary anchor text (brand, URL, generic, long-tail)
- ✅ Monitor Google Search Console for manual actions
- ✅ Disavow toxic links proactively
- ✅ Maintain consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across directories
