Frequently Asked Questions

What does NovaGenAI do?

NovaGenAI builds production-grade enterprise AI systems — from autonomous AI agents and conversational voice AI to computational biotech intelligence and custom enterprise software. We help businesses deploy AI that works in the real world, not just in demos.

What is enterprise AI?

Enterprise AI refers to artificial intelligence systems designed for business-critical operations. Unlike consumer AI tools, enterprise AI must be secure, scalable, reliable, and compliant with industry regulations. NovaGenAI specialises in building these production-grade systems.

Who is NovaGenAI?

NovaGenAI Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian enterprise AI company founded by Don Calaki in November 2025. Headquartered in Cyberjaya's Bio-X Centre, we are backed by MerakInvestments, CryoCord Group, and Brighton Healthcare Group.

What industries does NovaGenAI serve?

We serve healthcare, biotech, financial services, and manufacturing industries with tailored AI solutions including voice agents, predictive analytics, document intelligence, and custom AI-native software platforms.

Where is NovaGenAI located?

NovaGenAI is headquartered at Suite 1-1, 1st Floor, Bio-X Centre, Persiaran Cyberpoint Selatan, Cyber 8, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia — in the heart of Malaysia's technology corridor.

What AI technologies does NovaGenAI use?

We leverage the full NVIDIA ecosystem (NeMo, NIM, CUDA, TensorRT, Triton, RAPIDS, DGX Spark) alongside models from Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google. We deploy on cloud, on-premise, and hybrid infrastructure with partners including Google Cloud and AMD.
