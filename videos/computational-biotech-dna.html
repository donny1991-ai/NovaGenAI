Computational Biotech: Where AI Meets the Building Blocks of Life

Published 28 February 2026 · NovaGenAI

This video explores how NovaGenAI is pioneering the intersection of artificial intelligence and biology. Using custom AI models trained on biological datasets, we're making DNA, gene expression profiles, and cellular behaviour machine-readable — enabling breakthroughs in drug discovery, personalised medicine, and in-silico modelling.

Traditional biology generates vast amounts of unstructured data that humans can barely process. Large language models, originally designed for human language, can be adapted to read biological sequences — treating gene expression as sentences, cellular states as narratives. NovaGenAI builds these custom models for enterprise biotech and pharmaceutical clients.

Key topics covered: Cell2Sentence methodology, custom LLM training on biological data, stem cell data analysis, in-silico drug modelling, and on-premise deployment for regulated healthcare environments.
