Frequently Asked Questions

What makes an AI-powered website different from a regular website?

An AI-powered website has intelligence built into its core — not bolted on as a plugin. This includes AI chatbots that understand context and qualify leads, smart search that learns from user behaviour, personalised content that adapts to each visitor, and AI analytics that optimise conversion rates in real-time. The AI runs across the entire user journey, not just in a chat widget.

Why build a custom CRM instead of using Salesforce or HubSpot?

Off-the-shelf CRMs with AI plugins are limited by the platform's architecture. A ground-up AI-native CRM has intelligence embedded in every layer — lead scoring uses real-time ML models trained on your data, automated follow-ups are handled by AI agents across email, WhatsApp, and voice, and pipeline predictions are genuinely predictive rather than rule-based. You get a system designed around how your business actually works.

What is a local LLM deployment and why would my company need one?

A local LLM deployment runs a large language model on your own infrastructure — inside your building, on your servers. Nothing leaves your network. This is critical for organisations handling sensitive data where data sovereignty is non-negotiable. It also becomes cheaper long-term than paying per-API-call to cloud providers, especially at scale.

What NVIDIA hardware do you use for on-premise AI?

We deploy across the full NVIDIA ecosystem — DGX Spark, DGX systems, and custom GPU infrastructure. We use NeMo for model fine-tuning, NIM for optimised inference, CUDA for acceleration, and TensorRT for production deployment. The specific hardware depends on your model size, throughput requirements, and budget.

Can you deploy AI workloads across multiple cloud providers?

Yes. We build multi-cloud AI architectures across AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. This includes serverless AI endpoints, edge computing for low-latency inference, and auto-scaling infrastructure that handles traffic spikes without manual intervention. We go where your data and compliance requirements need us.
