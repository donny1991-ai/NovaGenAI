AI Agents vs Chatbots

Chatbots follow predefined conversation flows and answer from fixed FAQs. AI agents independently plan, reason, use tools, retrieve knowledge from multiple sources, and execute multi-step tasks. NovaGenAI's agents access business systems, make decisions within guardrails, coordinate with other agents, and learn from outcomes. Chatbots answer questions — agents do work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Malaysian companies build AI agents?
NovaGenAI builds autonomous AI agents and multi-agent orchestration platforms for enterprises in Malaysia. Our platform coordinates 40+ specialised agents for research, coding, analysis, compliance, and more — deployed on-premise or in cloud for regulated industries.
What are autonomous AI agents?
Autonomous AI agents are AI systems that independently plan, reason, use tools, and execute multi-step tasks without constant human supervision. Unlike chatbots (which follow scripted flows), AI agents make decisions, access business systems, retrieve knowledge, and coordinate with other agents to complete complex work.
What is multi-agent orchestration?
Multi-agent orchestration is the coordination of multiple specialised AI agents working together on complex tasks. NovaGenAI's platform manages 40+ agents with different expertise areas — assigning tasks, resolving dependencies, synthesising results, and applying quality validation — with human-in-the-loop approval for sensitive decisions.
How do AI agents integrate with existing systems?
NovaGenAI's AI agents integrate with enterprise systems (CRM, ERP, document management, databases, email, calendars) via APIs. They read data, execute actions, and write results back — functioning as an AI operating layer on top of existing infrastructure.
Are AI agents safe for enterprise use?
Yes. NovaGenAI's agents include guardrails (action boundaries), audit logging (every decision recorded), human-in-the-loop approval (for sensitive actions), and access controls (agents only access authorised systems). For regulated industries, agents can be deployed on-premise with full data sovereignty.
How long does it take to deploy AI agents?
Focused agent deployments take 3-5 days for an initial working system, with full production rollout in 4-8 weeks. Multi-agent orchestration platforms typically take 8-16 weeks depending on integration complexity.
