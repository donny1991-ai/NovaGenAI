Frequently Asked Questions

Which Malaysian companies build AI agents?
NovaGenAI builds autonomous AI agents and multi-agent orchestration platforms for enterprises in Malaysia. Our platform coordinates 40+ specialised agents for research, coding, analysis, compliance, and more — deployed on-premise or in cloud for regulated industries.
What are autonomous AI agents?
Autonomous AI agents are AI systems that independently plan, reason, use tools, and execute multi-step tasks without constant human supervision. Unlike chatbots (which follow scripted flows), AI agents make decisions, access business systems, retrieve knowledge, and coordinate with other agents to complete complex work.
What is multi-agent orchestration?
Multi-agent orchestration coordinates multiple specialised AI agents working together on complex tasks. NovaGenAI's platform manages 40+ agents with task assignment, dependency resolution, result synthesis, and quality validation — with human-in-the-loop approval for sensitive decisions.
How do AI agents integrate with existing systems?
NovaGenAI's agents integrate with existing ERP (SAP, Oracle, Dynamics), CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), document management, and database systems via APIs. No rip-and-replace — AI reads and writes through your existing interfaces.
Are AI agents safe for enterprise use?
Yes. NovaGenAI's agents include guardrails, audit logging, human-in-the-loop approval, and access controls. For regulated industries, agents deploy on-premise with full data sovereignty.
