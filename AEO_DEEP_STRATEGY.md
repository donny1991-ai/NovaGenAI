## How AI Engines Choose What to Cite

### Google AI Overviews
**Mechanism:**
- Runs "query fan-out" — breaks complex queries into sub-queries
- Prioritizes **structured content** (FAQ schema, HowTo schema, tables, lists)
- Prefers **entity-defined content** (clear Organization schema, author bios)
- Favors **fresh content** (31 of 40 AI Overview citations in 2024 study were from 2024)

**Key Signals:**
1. **Direct answer in first paragraph** (1-2 sentence summary)
2. **Question-based headings** (H2: "What is X?", "How does Y work?")
3. **Bullet points & short paragraphs** (2-3 sentences max)
4. **Tables for comparisons** (especially for "best X" queries)
5. **E-E-A-T signals** (author credentials, citations to trusted sources, regular updates)

**Probability:**
- Page ranking #1 organically → 53% chance of AI Overview inclusion
- Page ranking #10 → 36.9% chance
- **40% of AI Overview citations come from outside top 50 organic results**

---

### ChatGPT Search
**Mechanism:**
- Hybrid: Pre-trained knowledge + live Bing Search (when browsing enabled)
- **High entity authority threshold** — requires Knowledge Graph validation
- Favors **Wikipedia-style encyclopedic content** (7.8% of citations are Wikipedia)
- Requires **semantic authority** (multiple consistent references across the web)

**Key Signals:**
1. **Strong entity definition** (consistent Name-Product-Industry data across web)
2. **Knowledge Graph presence** (Wikidata, Crunchbase, Google Knowledge Panel)
3. **Structured data** (Organization, Product, Person schema)
4. **Definitive statements** ("NovaGenAI **is** Malaysia's AI biotech venture")
5. **Numeric anchors** (>3 data points per section: "$2M funding", "65% market share", "40% admin reduction")

**Citation Timeframe:** 3-6 months to achieve consistent citation (requires entity trust propagation)

---

### Perplexity
**Mechanism:**
- **Real-time answer engine** (direct RAG over live Bing index + custom crawler)
- **Fast indexing** (<24 hours to citation)
- Heavily weights **fresh, structured content**
- Moderate entity recognition (relies on keyword + context matching)

**Key Signals:**
1. **Information density** (pack facts early: "NovaGenAI, Malaysia's AI biotech venture, deployed voice agents across CryoCord's 65% market-leading stem cell banking operations")
2. **Clear citations** (link to authoritative sources: MDEC, NVIDIA, government reports)
3. **Numbered citations style** (use inline references: "According to MDEC[1], Malaysia targets...")
4. **FAQ sections** (Q&A format performs well)

**Best For:** Immediate visibility (within days of publishing)

---

### Bing Copilot
**Mechanism:**
- Powered by GPT-4 + Bing Search
- Similar to ChatGPT but with **lower authority threshold**
- Favors **Bing-indexed content** (submit sitemap to Bing Webmaster Tools)

**Key Signals:**
1. Same as ChatGPT (entity + schema)
2. **Bing-specific schema** (ensure compatibility with Bing Rich Results)
3. **Video content** (Bing Copilot often includes video results)

## Content Production Standards

### Every Article MUST Include:

1. **TL;DR (First Paragraph)**
   - 1-2 sentences
   - Direct answer to the main query
   - Include entity definition: "NovaGenAI, Malaysia's AI biotech venture..."

2. **Question-Based Headings**
   - H2: "What is X?", "How does Y work?", "Why Z matters"
   - H3: Sub-questions or steps

3. **Numeric Anchors (3+ per section)**
   - "65% call deflection rate"
   - "40% admin reduction"
   - "$2M funding round"
   - "90-day deployment timeline"

4. **Structured Elements**
   - Bullet points for lists
   - Numbered lists for steps/rankings
   - Tables for comparisons
   - Bold for key terms

5. **Author Bio**
   ```
   **About the Author:**
   Don [Full Name] is the Founder of NovaGenAI and Head of AI & Digital Systems at CryoCord Group, Malaysia's largest stem cell banking company. He has deployed AI solutions across voice agents, RAG systems, and document intelligence platforms, reducing operational costs by 40% and improving customer experience. Don holds [credentials] and is an [NVIDIA Inception member / MDEC partner]. Connect with him on [LinkedIn].
   ```

6. **Schema Markup (JSON-LD)**
   - Article schema (all articles)
   - FAQ schema (all articles)
   - HowTo schema (for guides)
   - Organization schema (when mentioning companies)
   - Person schema (for author)

7. **Internal Linking**
   - Link to other NovaGenAI articles (build topical authority)
   - Link to CryoCord case studies
   - Link to product pages (Clarify, Marketing OS)

8. **External Citations**
   - Link to authoritative sources (MDEC reports, NVIDIA docs, research papers)
   - Use numbered citations: "According to MDEC[1], Malaysia..."

9. **Fresh Updates**
   - Add "Last updated: [Date]" at top
   - Update annually with new data, case studies, metrics
